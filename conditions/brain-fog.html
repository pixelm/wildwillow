Brain fog

also called mental cloudiness

A heavy, sluggish feeling in the head — kids may seem zoned out, struggle to focus, or say their brain feels stuck. In Ayurveda this is medhya kshaya — a depletion of mental clarity, often from screen overload, poor sleep, or kapha excess. The aim is to clear the dullness and gently restore steady focus.

Kapha Mild Kids 10+ & adults

Right now

  • Drink a full glass of cold water, slowly
  • Step outside for 10 minutes of sunlight
  • Eat a protein-rich snack — nuts, cheese, eggs
  • 20 jumping jacks or a quick run
  • Close all screens for 20 minutes

Build the habit

  1. Day 1–3Hydrate, sleep, screen breaks
  2. Week 1Add brahmi morning
  3. Week 2–3Reassess; see a doctor if no shift
